About Us

We are a team of software engineers from Second Year Computer Science at the Universty of Lincoln. We were tasked with creating an artefact through our second semester that utilises machine learning. So, we created a dog breed recognition app using a large dataset of 120 different breeds of dogs and the ResNet50 machine learning model and produce this website free to be used by anyone, anywhere.
